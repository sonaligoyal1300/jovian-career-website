About Jovian

Jovian is a learning platform for data science and machine learning that offers practical, hands-on, and coding-focused courses. Aakash is the CEO and co-founder of Jovian. He holds a degree in computer science and mathematics from IIT Bombay. Aakash has worked as a software engineer at Twitter, Ireland & San Francisco. He was a world finalist at ACM ICPC, the olympics of programming. Courses taught by Aakash have 1.5 million+ views on YouTube.
